Lionel Messi is a football player from Argentina who plays for Inter Miami.
He has won the Ballon D'Or, the annual award given to the best player in the world, 8 times, 2022 FIFA World Cup winner and an Olympic gold medal winner in 2008.
He was born in 1987 in Rosario, Argentina's third-biggest city.
